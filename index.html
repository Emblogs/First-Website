Our Story

A Family Farm Built
on Passion & Trust

Era of Grace Farms was founded with one simple belief — that quality starts with care. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted pig farms in the region. Every animal we raise is treated with respect, fed naturally, and given the space to thrive.

Whether you're a family looking for fresh pork, a restaurant needing reliable supply, or a farmer seeking quality breeding stock — we are here to serve you with integrity and consistency.

🌿

Naturally Raised

Our pigs are raised on open pastures with 100% natural feed — no hormones, no shortcuts.

✅

Quality Guaranteed

Every pig that leaves our farm is healthy, well-fed and meets the highest standards.

🤝

Community Trusted
